Product Specialist (PMS) - APAC
Agilysys has been a leader in hospitality software for more than 40 years, delivering innovative cloud-
native SaaS and on-premises guest-centric technology solutions for gaming, hotels, resorts and cruise,
corporate foodservice management, restaurants, universities, healthcare, and sports and entertainment.
Agilysys offers the most comprehensive software solutions in the industry, including point-of-sale
(POS),property management (PMS),inventory and procurement,payments, and related applications,
to manage the entire guest journey. Agilysys is known for its leadership in hospitality, its broad product
offerings, and its customer-centric service. During recent years, Agilysys has made major investments in
R&D and has successfully modernized virtually all their longstanding trusted software solutions. Some of
the largest hospitality companies around the world use Agilysys solutions to help improve guest loyalty,
drive revenue growth, and increase operational efficiencies. Agilysys operates across the Americas,
Europe, the Middle East, Africa, Asia-Pacific & ANZ and India with Global headquarters located in
Alpharetta, GA.
ROLE SCOPE:
Reporting into the Technical Services Manager APAC (Singapore), the Product Specialist (PMS) APAC,
will be a techno functional Versa/PMS Suite expert that can manage and own Asia enterprise customers
for Agilysys. You will be empowered to drive our client implementations with the goal of both maximizing
the efficient use of resources and also client satisfaction.
The right candidate must be passionate about customer satisfaction but understand how to balance
customer needs. You will be able to clearly articulate client needs internally and represent the company
professionally externally. You will be responsible for the smooth and effective deployment of multiple
concurrent installations.
You have experience in handling large scale implementation projects, consulting for enterprise
customers taking them live with new systems and solutions.
You have strong PMS (Versa, Golf, Spa and Retail applications) functional skills with experience in project
management and business analysis.
The position will ensure standardization of project processes, process compliance, process
improvements, and successful delivery of projects to our customers. This position will also work closely with
other internal departments and senior management to continually help identify and assist in
improvements to the overall customer experience through improvements in installation, documentation,
Agilysys products, and ongoing support.
PRINCIPAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:
Project planning
Project progress monitoring and reporting, and client relations
Help products to meet industry needs.
Monitor market trends and develop alignment strategies.
Trade show and Marketing support.
